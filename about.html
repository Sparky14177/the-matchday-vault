The Matchday Vault

About This Project

Created and curated by Carl Anscombe (Sparky14177) — The Matchday Vault is a digital museum celebrating match‑worn football shirts and boots.

Built to be accessible via NFC, mobile, or desktop; works offline as a PWA; and optimized for elegant, distraction‑free viewing.

Launched 2025 · Design & build collaboration with ChatGPT (OpenAI)
